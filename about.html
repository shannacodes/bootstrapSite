ABOUT US

Rolling Pin Donuts is a local favorite in Ventura County. We have been serving warm, fresh and tasty donuts since 1994. Nestled in the city of Camarillo, CA, we are a family-owned business and enjoy crafting homemade donuts.

We bake in a traditional way using only the best quality ingredients.

If you see a line of people waiting, it must be for a reason. Come and find out! No dry donuts sitting all day here.
